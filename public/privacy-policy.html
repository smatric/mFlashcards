Privacy Policy for mFlashcards

Last updated: August 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

mFlashcards is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our flashcard service:

  • Google Account Information: Your name and email address when you sign in with Google OAuth
  • Google Drive Files: Access to Google Sheets files you explicitly select for use as flashcard decks
  • Usage Data: Local session statistics (cards studied, correct/incorrect answers) stored only in your browser

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Google OAuth
  • Display your name in the app interface
  • Access and read Google Sheets files you choose to use as flashcard decks
  • Automatically locate existing "mFlashcards" spreadsheets in your Google Drive

3. Data Storage and Security

  • No Server Storage: We do not store any of your personal data on our servers
  • Local Storage Only: Session statistics are stored locally in your browser and never transmitted
  • Google APIs: All interactions with your Google account are handled securely through Google's official APIs
  • HTTPS: All communications are encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Your data remains:

  • In your Google account (which you control)
  • In your local browser storage (which you can clear)
  • Never transmitted to or stored on our servers

5. Google OAuth and Permissions

mFlashcards requests the following Google permissions:

  • Google Drive: To search for and access spreadsheet files you select
  • Google Sheets: To read flashcard data from your chosen spreadsheets
  • User Profile: To display your name in the app interface

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your Google Account settings.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

mFlashcards uses:

  • OAuth Tokens: Temporarily stored to maintain your Google authentication session
  • Session Data: Local storage of your study session statistics and preferences
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Sign out of the application at any time
  • Revoke Google OAuth permissions through your Google Account
  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove session data
  • Stop using the service at any time

8. Children's Privacy

mFlashcards is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect additional personal information from children under 13. The app only accesses Google account information that parents/guardians have already approved for the child's Google account.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new privacy policy.

10. Open Source

mFlashcards is an open-source application. You can review our code and data handling practices at any time through our public repository.
